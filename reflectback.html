Reflectback Clothing
In Summer 2019, I completed Product Design coursework at Parsons School of Design in New York City. My main project was to look for design flaws in objects or processes around NYC. At first, I landed on improving the design of the garbage cans found throughout the city to have a smaller footprint. Through further research and ideation, I learned of the unexpectedly high injury rate among city sanitation workers. I then decided to shift my design focus to tackling that issue.

My thought and design process can be viewed below in the process book I created throughout the journey.
