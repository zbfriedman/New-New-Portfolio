WUNS UI Redesign
Student Project: The Washington University Nursery School was in the process of redesigning their website. Each student in my Introduction to Human-Computer Interaction class was tasked with creating our own version of the site. No colots, fonts, etc. were changed - we could only alter the layout of the pages and the content on the pages.

We then conducted user testing to determine the success rate of users attempting to complete various tasks on the new site vs. the original version in order to understand the effectiveness of our changes.
YEAR
2019
PLATFORM
Adobe InDesign
Outcome of the Changes
Baseline data was collected by asking users to complete various tasks on the orginal website. This data was calulated based on two factors: ability to complete the task and number of clicks (page changes) to complete the task.
