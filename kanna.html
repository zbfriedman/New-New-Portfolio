Kanna App
Student Project: I was given the task of designing an app that acts as a guide.

The app I created is called Kanna (which is Icelandic for "explore"). It is aimed towards tourists who are either currently in Iceland or planning a visit so they can become familair with the flora and fauna they may encounter. The data is categorized into four section: water, land, sky, and plants. The user can switch between the categories and select a specific animal or plant to learn more about through images, text, sounds, and maps.
