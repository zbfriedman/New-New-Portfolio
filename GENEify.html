GENEify Product Packaging
Student Project: In my Digital Design class, I was tasked with creating product packaging for any household object and chose to design a box for family genealogy tests. The light blue background targeted a younger audience, and the web-like patterns illustrated the networks discovered when learning about family histories.
YEAR
2019
PLATFORM
Adobe Illustrator
Additional design directions
I considered numerous possible designs for the packaging of the product. I wanted to stick with something that depicts the interconnectivity of family trees. This is why I played around with options like network webs and DNA helixes.
