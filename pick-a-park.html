Pick a Park Website
Student Project: I was given the task of creating an interface for a webstite that contains an archive of material.

The website and experience that I created takes a simple approach of providing users with data about parks to encourage travel to and appreciation of the country's beauty. The user can easily be "quizzed" so the system can determine which of the 62 national parks are within their reach. The parks are sorted by best season to visit, region, popular activities, and park types. The users are able to sort through their results as they view information and images about each park.
